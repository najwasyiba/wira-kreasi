    Welcome to PT. Wira Kreasi Usaha

    PT. Wira Kreasi Usaha (WKU) is an authorized employment service company located in West Java, Indonesia, with a renewed license no. 608/MEN/2016. Established in 1996, WKU has dedicated its strategy, effort, and resources to provide competent and qualified workers; since its inception, WKU has placed close to 100,000 workers all over the world.
